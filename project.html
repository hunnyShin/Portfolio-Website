ELEJEWEL - Jewerly Store App

Overview

The final goal of outstanding fashion is wearing jewelry. ELEJEWEL is an online Jewelry store, and the app provides users to shop for unique handmade jewelry. All users can purchase their desired jewelry only one click.

Guiding Priniciple

"Gentle" "Confidence" "Trust"

Low and Mid Fidelity

User Story

1. As a user, I need to find the best products, so that I can get what kind of designs are popular in the app.

2. As a new user, I want to look through all screens and products without login, so that I define the app store is my favorite or not.

3. As a user, I need to contact them quickly if my item has a problem that I bought, so that I can discuss it for returning or refunding.

User Testing Notes & Results

Story 1 - Access Best Products

Elisa - The app has a clear category page on the shop screen. Generally, your design is organized very well, so users can find what they want, and one of the examples is finding the best products screen. However, some words are too small to read. I would you like to fix it bigger, for example, "Free Shipping" and "Heart rates."


Edward - 1: You can emphasize some important words like "Best" to "BEST" and "Sale" to "SALE." It helps attract users' eyes more clearly.

Story 2 - Access without Login

Megan - The primary logo is essential to imprint users understand what the brand is at the first moment. So, I want you to put the logo on the flash screen.


Story 3 - Access to Customer Service

Elisa - Your Text size is still not big enough. Phones' screens are bigger and bigger, so less than size 17 is inappropriate.

Logo

ELEJEWEL

ELEJEWEL has compounded two words "Elegant" and "Jewelry." Simply it means the ELEJEWEL brand provides elegant jewelry. The round background feels sleek and stylish, and Intaglio letters emphasize the luxury of typography.

Color

There are three colors ELEJEWEL uses: Black, Gold, and Beige. The black color is slightly lighter than the natural black, which is chic and warm, Gold is one of the jewelry colors that is enthusiastic and luxurious, and Beige is also bright and soft. These three colors bring the best synergy effect together.
